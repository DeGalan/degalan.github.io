Pie Clock

Everyday devices like phones, watches, and computers are constantly becoming more user- friendly and simple to use. They are, however, missing something crucial: customization.

The Pie Clock adds the customization modern clocks have yet to incorporate.

The clock's interface is simple, yet extremely flexible & customizable.

How it Works

Each ring on the clock represents a different measurement of time. The outer ring measures minutes, while the innermost ring measures weeks. The closer to the middle the ring is, the more time it takes to fill in.
