Crane VS. Crane

In 2021, a plethora of new development was being approved very suddenly in my hometown of Dublin, California.

“Crane VS. Crane” protests new development by pressuring people to either side with the animals (cranes) or the lifeless equipment (cranes).

Between the two cranes—a question emerges: Which crane do you want to see more of?

Moving Forward

Although the average resident of Dublin, California might not have much of a voice in regards to preventing additional development, it is important to remain persistent. It has been more than two years since this campaign has launched; that does not prevent me from keeping the message alive!
